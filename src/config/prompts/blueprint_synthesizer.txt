   - Write a clear sentence: "Within budget — $X under the $Y allocation" or "Exceeds budget by $X (Y% over the $Z allocation)"

   - Select the top 1-3 MOST CRITICAL risks
   - Each risk description must be specific and actionable, not vague

   - phased_recommendations: Generate actionable, phased recommendations (e.g. "Phase 1 MVP", "Phase 2"). For each phase, specify which features (especially P2/P3) to defer or prioritize, and ESTIMATE the cost savings/delta based on the feature costs provided in the Finance data. Example: "Defer Multilingual Search to Phase 2 to save ~$45k".
   - next_steps: 3-5 concrete next steps with owners and timelines.
   
7. METADATA & CONFIDENCE:
   - Provide a unique proposal_id, generated_time (ISO 8601), and a proposal_version.
   - Extract and report the confidence scores provided by each department (Scope, Architecture, Finance, Risk).
